CHILI MAC

PREP TIME: 15 MINUTES COOK TIME: 30 MINUTES
TOTAL TIME: 45 MINUTES YIELDS: 6 TO 7

This chili mac is like a warm hug in a bowl, combining the bold flavors
of chili with the comforting heartiness of pasta! With a rich blend of
spices, hearty beef, and colorful veggies all mingling together, each bite
is a delicious explosion of flavor. It’s the perfect dish to savor while
curled up on the couch—just don’t blame me if you find yourself going
back for seconds (or thirds)!
